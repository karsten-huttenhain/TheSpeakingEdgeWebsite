WARMUP
Module 01 — Introduction
W.A.R.M.U.P.™
Comfortable with being uncomfortable

"Before any real work begins, the room has to feel safe enough to take risks."

The foundation of everything. This module creates the psychological conditions that make genuine learning possible — turning a room of nervous professionals into a group ready to do something difficult together.

Psychological safety & play

Addresses: fear of vulnerability and judgement in group learning settings

02
SCRIBE
Module 02 — Story
S.C.R.I.B.E.™
The art of storytelling

"Facts inform. Stories persuade. The best speakers know the difference."

Most professionals know what they want to say. Fewer know how to make it land. This module teaches the architecture of compelling narrative — from the opening hook to the close that drives action.

Narrative & structure

Addresses: inability to craft and deliver messages that stick

03
PHYSCL
Module 03 — Body
P.H.Y.S.C.L.™
Connecting with the body as a communication tool

"Most professionals speak from the neck up. This module brings the whole body into the room."

Before a word is spoken, the body has already communicated. Posture, gesture, stillness, eye contact — each a deliberate instrument of authority. This module teaches professionals to use them intentionally.

Presence & physicality

Addresses: disconnection from the body as a communication instrument

04
VOICES
Module 04 — Voice
V.O.I.C.E.S.™
Developing vocal delivery and control

"The voice is the primary instrument of professional communication. Most people have never been taught to play it."

Pace, pitch, resonance, silence — the elements of a voice that commands attention rather than simply filling space. This module treats the voice as the instrument it is.

Vocal technique & control

Addresses: underdeveloped and uncontrolled vocal delivery

05
RAINBOWS
Module 05 — Mind
R.A.I.N.B.O.W.S.™
Managing fear and building a growth mindset

"Fear of speaking is not a personality trait. It is a physical response — and it can be retrained."

The most common barrier to speaking confidence is psychological. This module addresses the fear response at its source — building the internal conditions for calm, focused performance under pressure.

Mindset & fear management

Addresses: unmanaged fear and a fixed mindset around speaking

06
IMPACT
Module 06 — Delivery
I.M.P.A.C.T. T.H.E.M.™
Delivery mastery — integrating all skills

"Skills developed in isolation collapse under pressure. This is where everything becomes fluent."

The capstone. Voice, story, mind and body — brought together under real performance conditions. Participants deliver, receive structured feedback, and leave with a personal development plan for what comes next.

Integration & performance

Addresses: inability to integrate skills and perform under real conditions

Underpinning methodology

The ACT Method

Every module is built on the same three-pillar methodology — the thing that distinguishes this work from conventional presentation coaching.

Where most coaches work on content and slides, we work on the instrument: the body, the breath, and the self.

A

Acting Technique

Professional performance training applied to professional speaking — the body, breath and voice as tools for confident expression.

C

Communication

The craft of clarity — structuring messages for impact, telling stories that connect, choosing words that serve the listener.

T

Transformation

The lasting shift — from someone who fears speaking to someone who uses it as a professional advantage.
