Appendix

Worksheets & Quick Reference.

Pre-meeting prep sheet

Fill this in before any meeting where you need to be heard. Inputs are session-only and cleared when you close the tab.

Tap any phrase to copy it to your clipboard.

When you need a beat to think
"Let me think about it for a second." "I want to give you a real answer rather than a fast one." "Give me thirty seconds to land this."
When you don't know
"I don't know yet — here's how I'd find out." "That's outside what I've looked at closely. What I can speak to is…" "I don't have data on that, but my read on it is…"
When you're interrupted
"Hold on — let me finish my thought, and then I want to hear you." "I wasn't quite done. Give me thirty seconds." "One moment — the part I was getting to is the part that matters."
When pushback turns personal
"I want to make sure we're still discussing the work and not something else." "Let me address what you actually asked." "I'm happy to keep going on this — calmly."
