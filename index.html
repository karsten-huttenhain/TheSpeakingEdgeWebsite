Why it works

This is not a presentation tips course

Most speaking coaching addresses symptoms. We address the source. Fear of speaking lives in the body — in shallow breath, locked posture, and a voice that hides. The Speaking Edge works at that level: physical, specific, and grounded in professional performance technique.

Our founder Karsten Huttenhain brings nearly two decades as a theatre and screen actor to corporate coaching. We teach you to inhabit your voice, command your body, and own your story.

The PERFORM Framework

Eight weeks. Seven pillars. One transformation.

Every module builds on the last — from the physiological foundations of breath and voice, through to integrated performance under live interview pressure.

Week 1P
Presence
Posture, physical command, spatial ownership — the GEM method.
Week 2E
Expression
Voice production, pitch, pace, clarity and authority.
Week 3R
Respiration
Diaphragmatic breathing and nervous system regulation.
Week 4F
Formal Writing
CVs, cover letters, professional email — confidence on the page.
Week 5O
Ownership
Silence, mindset, nerves and personal narrative.
Week 6R
Relationship
Audience connection, trust-building, reading a room.
Wks 7–8M
Mastery
Live mock interviews — 1:1, panel and group formats.
